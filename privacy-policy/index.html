OXIDIZE — Privacy Policy

What We Collect

Oxidize collects only the data necessary to provide the workout tracking service:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Workout sessions (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Body weight measurements
  • Saved routines and user preferences

HealthKit

If you grant permission, Oxidize reads and writes body weight data to Apple Health and logs completed workouts. HealthKit data is never sent to external servers — it stays on your device and in Apple Health.

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database with row-level security. Each user can only access their own data. Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. The only external services used are:

  • Supabase (database and authentication)
  • Google Gemini (AI routine generation — no personal data is sent, only workout preferences)

Data Deletion

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Upon request, all data will be permanently removed from our servers.
